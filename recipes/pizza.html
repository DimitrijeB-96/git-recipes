Pizza

This is traditional Italian pizza!

Ingredients:

  • Dough for pizza
  • Tomato paste
  • Salami
  • Cheese
  • Sosage(Kulen)
  • Olive

Steps:

  1. Make/Buy dough for pizza
  2. Make curcle shape of dough
  3. Put Tomato paste on top of dough
  4. Evenly spread Salami over the tomato paste
  5. Grate cheese on top of the Salami
  6. Place Kulen and Olives on top of the cheese
  7. Bake your pizza in oven
