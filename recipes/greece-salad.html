Greece salad

Greece salad make on a Serbian way!

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive
  • Olive oil
  • onions

Steps:

  1. Chop Tomatoes, cucumbers and onions
  2. Mix them together
  3. Put mix in the bowl
  4. Chop Feta cheese
  5. Put Olives
  6. Pour Olive oil on top
  7. Mix all together
